# How to Use the Extension - Quick Guide

## ⚠️ **Important First Step**

Before using the extension, you MUST:

1. **Go to Instagram.com** in your browser
2. **Navigate to YOUR profile page** by clicking on your profile picture or going to `instagram.com/yourusername`
3. Make sure you can see your follower/following counts on the page
4. **THEN** click the extension icon and try the analysis

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Finding Non-Followers

1. ✅ **Go to your profile**: `instagram.com/yourusername`
2. ✅ **Wait for page to fully load** (you should see your posts grid)
3. ✅ **Click the extension icon** in Chrome toolbar
4. ✅ **Click "Find Non-Followers"** button
5. ⏳ **Wait patiently** - this takes 2-5 minutes depending on how many people you follow
6. 📊 **View results**

### Common Issues

| Error Message | Solution |
|---------------|----------|
| "Please navigate to your profile first" | Go to instagram.com/yourusername before clicking analyze |
| "Element [role=dialog] not found" | Make sure you're on your profile page, not home feed |
| "Could not find following button" | Refresh the Instagram page and make sure you're logged in |

## What's Happening Behind the Scenes

When you click analyze:
1. Extension finds your "following" button and clicks it
2. Waits for the popup dialog to appear
3. Scrolls through the entire list to load all users
4. Does the same for "followers"
5. Compares the two lists
6. Shows you who doesn't follow back

## Troubleshooting

If it's not working:
1. **Reload the extension**: Go to `chrome://extensions/`, find Instagram Analytics Pro, click reload
2. **Refresh Instagram**: Press F5 or Cmd+R on the Instagram page
3. **Check you're on your profile**: URL should be `instagram.com/yourusername`
4. **Try again**: Click the extension icon and analyze again
